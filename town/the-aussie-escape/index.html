A sun-bleached outback souvenir shop that crams the Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge, and a church spire into one gloriously kitschy roadside attraction. White Opera House sails sprout from the rear roof corner, a Harbour Bridge arch spans the verandah entrance, and an Adelaide church spire guards the front. A kangaroo poses out front, a koala clings to the eucalyptus tree, and a platypus wanders the path. Inside: Vegemite jars, Akubra hats, a boomerang on the wall, and the smell of Fair Dinkum Souvenirs.

🏘️ The Aiffel Tower

Rising gracefully into the sky, this bronze-and-gold lattice tower is a loving tribute to the Eiffel Tower. Four tapered legs join at sweeping parabolic arches; three tiered platforms host a Parisian café, a gallery, and a panoramic observation deck. A constellation of golden sparkle lights makes it shimmer like a jewel at dusk — an homage to beauty, courage, and artistry.
